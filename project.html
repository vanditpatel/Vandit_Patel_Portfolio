Autonomous UAV Swarm for Extensive Surveillance

In the "Autonomous Swarm of Drones for Survey and Rescue" project, I took the lead in a collaborative effort, weaving together the intricacies of Mavlink, Jetson Nano, Pixhawk, and advanced machine learning (ML).

Key Contributions:

  • Object Detection and Localization: Engineered a robust software system enabling the main drone to detect people/pedestrians, pinpoint their coordinates, and transmit this crucial information to microdrones.
  • Microdrone Closeup Investigation: Implemented a sophisticated mechanism where microdrones autonomously navigated to the designated coordinates for a closeup look. This feature allowed for a detailed examination of the detected individuals.
  • Frames Transmission and Approval Workflow: Orchestrated the process of transmitting frames captured by microdrones to the ground station for further analysis. Upon reaching the ground station, the frames awaited approval. Once approval was received, a confirmation signal was sent to the main drone, indicating that the closeup investigation was completed successfully.

Water Quality Monitoring using IoT

In a collaborative effort with a dedicated team, I contributed to the design and construction of a Water Quality Monitoring prototype. This project leveraged Arduino and ESP8266 technologies to create a robust system for continuous measurement of essential water elements, including pH, Turbidity, and Total Dissolved Solids (TDS).

Key Contributions:

  • Constant Measurement System: Implemented a reliable system using Arduino to continuously measure water elements, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of water quality.
  • Real-time Data Transmission: Successfully integrated ESP8266 to transmit collected data to the ThingSpeak platform. This allowed for real-time monitoring of water quality, providing valuable insights.
  • Threshold-Based Alerts: Developed a smart mechanism for sending emails and notifications when water quality surpassed predetermined threshold values. This feature ensured timely awareness and response to potential water quality issues.

Funding Acknowledgment:

  • Secured a significant funding grant of 1,00,000 INR from Indus University, underscoring the importance and impact of our Water Quality Monitoring project.
